🛠 Getting Started Tips

Materials for each Render Pipeline are located in the /Materials folder

Use the correct material for URP, HDRP, or Built-in RP depending on your project

Tree prefabs are located under /Prefabs sorted by category (Pine, Palm, Woodlands)
